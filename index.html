FAQ

Questions you might have

How does Trimp work?

We work with you to deeply understand your taste and budget, then custom-create the clothing styles you choose in the most timely, stressless way possible.

What if I don't have the money upfront?

We work out a flexible, interest-free installment plan to take the pain out of the payment process.

What happens if I don't like the final product?

We will retrieve the clothing and return your money back 100%. We will proactively reach out to offer other options to help you achieve your goal.

What do I get for referring someone?

You win Trimp points. Your trimp points can be convereted into cash that you can use to sew more clothes.
